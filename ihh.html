Integrated Health Home

Our Integrated Health Home (IHH) is a team of professionals, including family and peer support services, that work together to provide whole-person, patient-centered, coordinated care for adults with a serious mental illness (SMI) and children with a serious emotional disturbance (SED). Our Care Coordinators help IHH members to meet individual goals related to their healthcare needs- and will work with members' to provide information and resources required to assist members with healthy living.

Who's eligible to receive this service?

This service is available to children and adults enrolled in Iowa Medicaid and residing in one of the above listed counties.

Where can I get this service?

Page, Fremont, & Montgomery Counties
